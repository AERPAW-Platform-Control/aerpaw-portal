Join the AERPAW Users Forum

If you use AERPAW, or intend to use it, or are just interested in staying in touch with AERPAW preparatory to potentially using it, please join this forum. The AERPAW team will post informational messages and announcements here that are relevant for users of AERPAW. Specific questions about AERPAW experiments or usage may also be posted here, and may receive responses from the AERPAW team, or other users.

However, please note that:
  • (i) this is not the appropriate source to gather general information about AERPAW - navigate to the User Manual at aerpaw.org for that;
  • (ii) this is not the forum for requesting specific help for issues with specific experiments - please use one of the following methods instead:
    Aerpaw Office Hours - one-on-one help session Aerpaw Bug Bash - drop-in help session Send us an email - reach out to aerpaw-contact@ncsu.edu
